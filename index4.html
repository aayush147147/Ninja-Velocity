1. DATA WE COLLECT

We collect your username, best score, and coin balance stored locally on your device. Scores may be shared to our global leaderboard (Firebase) to enable rankings.


2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Your data is used solely to provide game features — leaderboard rankings, saving your progress, and skin ownership. We do not sell your data to any third party.

This game uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve personalised ads. You can opt out at google.com/settings/ads.

We use your browser's localStorage to save game progress (score, coins, settings). No third-party tracking cookies are set by us directly.


5. CHILDREN

This game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

📖 HOW TO PLAY

BECOME THE ULTIMATE NINJA
◀ ▶  SWITCH LANES
Tap the LEFT or RIGHT buttons on screen, or swipe left/right. Move sideways to dodge obstacles coming in your lane.
↑  JUMP
Tap the JUMP button or swipe up. Jump over obstacles in your lane. Tap jump again while in the air for a double-jump!
🪙  COLLECT COINS
Gold coins appear along the road. Run through them to collect. Coins buy skins, worlds and accessories in the shop.
🚧  OBSTACLES
Wall — red brick wall, dodge sideways.
Rock — tumbling boulder, dodge sideways.
Spike — sharp spikes on ground, jump or dodge.
Barrel — explosive red barrel, dodge sideways.
Crate — wooden box, dodge sideways.
⚡  POWER-UPS
Glowing orbs give you powers: Super Jump, Magnet (attracts coins), Glide, Speed Boost, Coin Rush ×3, Time Slow, Invincible, Score ×2.
🔥  TIPS
• The game speeds up every 400m — stay sharp!
• Buy shields from skins for extra hits
• Spin the wheel every 3 hours for free coins
• Complete daily missions for bonus coins
• Play every day for streak bonuses
